Schools of fish and other flocking schemes are called boids, and since the 80s, there have been scientists looking into the patterns that they follow

there are 3 main things that they do to form schools:

  • following the average of those nearby (Alignment)
  • trying to get into the center of the school(cohesion)
  • trying to not crowd in too closely to others (seperation)

so this program gives those 3 simulations, and a slider to set the priority of each rule

More on this by Craig Reynolds: https://www.red3d.com/cwr/boids/
