100 Days - 100 Projects

Hello, My name is Brian Fairbanks. Thank you for checking out my 100 days of coding project!

In an attempt to work on many different things, and expand my web development abilities, I have started this page to write 1 project each day for 100 days.

Admittedly, some of these projects have not turned out quite as well as I would have liked -- They may have a bug or 2 in the UI, or may not be 100% finished, such as with the Pong demonstration. That is the risk I take when I alot myself only 1 day for each.
